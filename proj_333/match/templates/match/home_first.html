Welcome to TigerMingle!
You can wait for someone to add you to a group.
You can make a new group.


TigerMingle allows users to indicate preferences or characteristics on the basis of which they can choose to be matched. These preferences are then used by an algorithm in the backend which matches up people who are similar. If you want preference-based matching, you can indicate that by going to your profile and checking the tick-box at the bottom which says "I want preference-based matching."
